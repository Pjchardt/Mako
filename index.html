Summon digital spirits


Three particpants enter a digital void between the nodes of our network. Here they summon spirits that reside within the digital realm of data that surrounds us. Participants change the flow of ddata around them ny interfacing with artificats of an alien civilization that allows different frequencies to resonate in the void and call upon its inhabitants. Using VR headsets and headphones, the particpiants can witness the spirit world of our devices.
