Mako


Summon digital spirits


Three participants enter the digital realm through VR devices. Here they summon spirits that reside within the countless data of the 21st century by interfacing with artifacts of an alien civilization.

How it works


Three participants sit on the floor in triangle formation. Each participant wears a GearVR headset and headphones. In their lap they hold a custom input device with two IR sensors that detect proximity.

The three custom input devices send data to an Arduino which communicates with a Unity application running on a PC, via USB. Unity then syncs the data over a local wireless network with the three GearVR devices.

Inside the experience, participants see each other, represented as animal masks. Head rotation and voice data is synced over the network, isolating the participants from the analog world and building a sense of social presence inside the digital space. As participants interact with their input devices, nodes of light pulse and connect into rings of energy. Participants engage in a post modern ritual to call forth the Akashic record of the digital age.
